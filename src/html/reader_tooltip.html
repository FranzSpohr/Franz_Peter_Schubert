Navigate between pages by  left clicking on arrows
at the edges of the screen.

Left click on the dots  near the bottom of the screen
to jump between pages.

  		•  Esc Key: Return to Reader
  		• Double Left Click: Zoom in
  		• Left Click &
    Mouse Drag:    		 Scroll document
  		• Right Click: Zoom out
  		• Right Click:
    (if on default zoom level)     		Return to Reader
  		• Up Arrow Key: Scroll up
  		• Down Arrow Key: Scroll down
  		• Left Arrow Key: Previous page
  		• Right Arrow Key: Next page
